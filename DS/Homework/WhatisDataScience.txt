Data Science is the act of taking information and interpreting trends within the information, then finding clear and concise ways to communicate a story with the information or data provided. To be a data scientist is to be a story teller, taking complex amounts of information, and utilizing computation to interpret and better understand that information with the intent of communicating trends and important information ethically to others in a way that they can understand and build upon. 

Simply put, Data Science is process of information being collected and then fed into the conflux of relevant knowledge applied using scientific methods, mathematical interpretation, and machine learning; all turned into a clearly communicated story for all to understand.
